Billboard Music Awards 2024: A Night of Unforgettable Hits and Global Sounds

NEWS Dec 13, 2024
Billboard Music Awards 2024 Nominees

The 2024 Billboard Music Awards took center stage on December 12, showcasing a vibrant celebration of the year’s most influential music and artists. Held in Los Angeles and hosted by the charismatic Michelle Buteau, the star-studded event highlighted a diverse array of talents and genres. Taylor Swift was the night’s big winner, securing awards for Top Artist and Top Billboard 200 Album for her reimagined 1989 (Taylor’s Version). SZA and Olivia Rodrigo also claimed their share of glory, with SZA earning Top R&B Artist and Olivia snagging Top Hot 100 Song for her emotional hit, “Vampire.”

The performances electrified the stage, from Doja Cat’s edgy rendition of “Paint the Town Red” to Stray Kids’ high-octane K-pop showcase with “S-Class.” A standout moment was The Weeknd and BLACKPINK’s Rosé teaming up for a captivating duet, merging genres seamlessly.

This year also emphasized global influence, awarding Burna Boy Top Global Artist (Excluding U.S.), a testament to Afrobeats’ growing impact worldwide. By celebrating both mainstream and international artistry, the 2024 Billboard Music Awards highlighted the boundless reach of music today.

Beyond the awards and performances, the evening carried a spirit of innovation. From emerging artists carving out new spaces to veterans reinventing themselves, the 2024 Billboard Music Awards reflected an industry in motion—bold, inclusive, and brimming with creativity. It was a night to remember, proving once again that music is the universal language that brings us all together. With its mix of star power, heartfelt moments, and global flair, this year’s Billboard Music Awards didn’t just highlight the best in music—it set the stage for what’s to come in 2025.
