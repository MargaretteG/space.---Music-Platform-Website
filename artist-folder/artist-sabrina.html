Sabrina Carpenter

AKA: Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter
POP R&B SYNTH-POP 2011-Present

Sabrina Carpenter is a versatile artist renowned for her talent as a singer, songwriter, and actress. Born on May 11, 1999, in Lehigh Valley, Pennsylvania, Sabrina's journey into the entertainment industry began at a young age. Her dedication to the arts was evident when she placed third in a singing contest hosted by Miley Cyrus on Disney Channel's The Next Miley Cyrus Project.

Sabrina made her acting debut in a guest role on the hit crime drama series Law & Order: SVU in 2011. Her breakout role came in 2014 when she starred as Maya Hart, the witty and rebellious best friend on Disney Channel’s popular series Girl Meets World. This role catapulted her into the spotlight and earned her a devoted fanbase.

Sabrina’s musical debut was equally impressive. She released her first single, “Can’t Blame a Girl for Trying,” in 2014 under Hollywood Records. The song won over listeners with its heartfelt lyrics and catchy melody, establishing Sabrina as a promising young artist.

In addition to her music and acting projects, Sabrina has ventured into the fashion world, becoming a muse for several high-profile brands. Her world tours and live performances showcase her dynamic stage presence, earning her a place among pop's rising stars.

Sabrina Carpenter’s ability to seamlessly transition between heartfelt ballads and upbeat pop anthems sets her apart. Her relatable lyrics and authenticity resonate with fans worldwide, making her a role model for aspiring artists.
